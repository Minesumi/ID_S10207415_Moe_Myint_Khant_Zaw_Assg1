MrBeast

What Is MrBeast?

MrBeast is a YouTube channel with about 47 million subscribers. It is the 20th most subscribed youtube channel right now in November 2020. This channel became famous because the channel owner gives away nearly all the money he makes from this channel and then makes content videos about how he gives money away. The channel also have 12 sub-channels such as MrBeast Gaming, Beast Reacts, etc.

History Of MrBeast

Since 2012, a 13 years old boy named Jimmy Donaldson uploaded his videos of gaming on the YouTube channel named "MrBeast6000". Some of his videos were clickbaits at that time. He did not get much views until 2017, when he uploaded a video of counting to 100,000 in one sitting which took him about 40 hours. Then, after he became a little famous, he made videos about experiments that are not done by others, which made his channel even more popular. And then, he made videos about how he gives away the money he makes from YouTube and from his sponsors.

Content And Style

He makes most of his videos with a group of his friends. He makes attention-grabbing videos such as donating a large amount of money to individuals. He also made difficult stunts auch as staying underwater for 24 hours, staying on a deserted island for 24 hours, staying in the desert for 24 hours and so on. Then, he made videos on the challenges where he made his contestants compete for a million dollars, for an island, for a Lamborghini, for a house and so on. He grabbed most of the audiences' attention by making contents about giving his money away. A typical video involves Donaldson giving away large sums of money, such as giving $100,000 worth of items to homeless shelters in December 2018, donating $32,000 to the Veterans Army Wounded Warrior Program, $70,000 to Saint Jude Children's Research Hospital, and $10,000 to a local animal shelter in Los Angeles.

The following video, which was uploaded in 2018, is his most popular video where he filled the backyard of his friend's house with 100 million orbeez and the video got over 100 million views.

Current Status

Now, Donaldson has 695 videos on "MrBeast", his main channel, which has 46.9 million subscribers. His second largest channel, "MrBeast Gaming" also has 10.7 million subscribers with just 78 videos. Donaldson, the owner of MrBeast channel, dropped out of college in order to pursue a full-time career as a YouTuber. He is now 22 years old and became one of the biggest YouTuber in the world. His main channel is now one of the largest channel in the world after 9 years of effort.
